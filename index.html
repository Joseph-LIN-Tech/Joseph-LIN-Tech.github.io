Guixu Lin

Guixu Lin is a 3rd year Ph.D. student at the Graduate School of Information Science and Technology (IST), the University of Tokyo (Utokyo), advised by Prof. Yinqiang Zheng.
Currently, he is a research intern in Tencent. Guixu Lin receieved his Master degree from Utokyo in March 2023, advised by Prof. Ryosuke Shibasaki. Prior to Utokyo, Guixu Lin worked as a Reaserach Assistant at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of Southern University of Science and Technology (Sustech), supervised by Prof. Xuan Song from 2019 to 2021. And he received his Bachelor degree in South China University of Technology (SCUT).

Guixu Lin is generally interested in computer vision, image and video processing. His current research interest lies in video generation, event-cameras-based image and video restoration. Feel free to contact Guixu Lin via his email!
